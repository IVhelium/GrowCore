About Us

We are GrowCore — a startup founded in 2025 with a mission to make smart greenhouse technology accessible to everyone.

We design and develop automated home greenhouse systems that combine climate control, smart monitoring, and efficient resource management in one integrated solution

Our goal is to help people grow healthier plants with less effort by using modern electronics, automation, and data-driven control. GrowCore systems monitor temperature, humidity, soil conditions, and lighting, allowing users to create the ideal environment for plant growth.

As a young and innovative startup, we focus on sustainability, simplicity, and reliability. We believe that the future of home gardening lies in smart automation and conscious resource use.

GrowCore is more than just a product — it's a step toward smarter, greener living.

Our Services

Our solutions, services, and products are designed to be accessible worldwide, enabling smart and sustainable plant growth wherever you are.
